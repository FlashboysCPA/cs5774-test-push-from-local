Rules and Regulations

Mandated by Falls Church Dog Park, Punishable by Falls Church

  • All dogs must be licensed and vaccinated before entering the Dog Park.
  • Both rabies vaccinations tags and Dog License tags are required to be secured on your dog at all times.
  • No dogs less than four (4) months of age shall be permitted in a Dog Park.
  • Handlers / guardians are responsible for removing dog waste from the Park and disposing it in a proper receptacle.
  • Dogs must be leashed when entering and exiting the Dog Park.
  • Violations of the leash law, running at large law and not picking up after your dog can result in a summons to appear in court and a $100.00 fine.
